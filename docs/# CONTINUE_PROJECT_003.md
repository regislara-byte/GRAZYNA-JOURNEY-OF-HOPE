# CONTINUE PROJECT 003

## Project

**GRAZYNA-JOURNEY-OF-HOPE**

---

# Objective

Refine the Hero section into a premium documentary experience.

This is **not** a redesign.

The existing layout, typography hierarchy, navigation, buttons, and content are already approved.

This implementation focuses exclusively on elevating the visual quality through **Visual Engineering**.

The final result should feel timeless, cinematic, and emotionally powerful while remaining restrained and believable.

---

# Design Philosophy

The Hero should immediately communicate:

* Humanity
* Hope
* Dignity
* Resilience

The portrait should feel like the opening frame of a documentary rather than a marketing website.

Think:

* Apple keynote craftsmanship
* A24 documentary cinematography
* Premium editorial photography
* Museum-quality exhibition poster

The experience should invite the visitor into Grażyna's story before asking for any action.

---

# Hero Direction

Maintain the current Hero composition.

Do not redesign the layout.

Do not move content blocks.

Do not replace typography.

Do not change navigation.

Instead, enhance the scene through light, depth, material, and atmosphere.

The Hero should feel premium rather than flat.

Apply subtle cinematic specular highlights and soft directional lighting.

Do **not** increase brightness.

Instead create depth through:

* soft rim lighting
* subtle metallic typography highlights
* low-opacity radial light bloom
* glossy hair reflections
* premium glass-like button highlights
* soft volumetric lighting
* layered shadows

The overall mood remains dark documentary, with luxury editorial polish.

Avoid:

* neon effects
* excessive glow
* unrealistic reflections
* chrome-like typography
* over-sharpening

Luxury.

Restrained.

Believable.

---

# Documentary Atmosphere

Maintain the darker cinematic mood.

Increase facial separation only enough that:

* eyes become more readable
* smile becomes more readable

The portrait must never become overly bright.

Typography remains integrated into the skin.

The portrait should continue feeling engraved rather than floating.

Hair reflections should remain subtle.

The Hero should preserve mystery while improving clarity.

---

# Material Language

The interface should communicate physical material.

Examples:

Typography

* brushed silver
* subtle metallic sheen
* premium editorial finish

Buttons

* premium glass surface
* soft inset highlights
* restrained reflections

Lighting

* ambient volumetric atmosphere
* layered vignette
* believable cinematic gradients

Everything should feel physically lit instead of digitally glowing.

---

# Visual Engineering Principles

Every lighting effect must serve a purpose.

Light separates.

Shadow creates depth.

Material creates realism.

Atmosphere creates emotion.

Nothing exists purely as decoration.

Each visual layer should reinforce the documentary narrative.

---

# Identity Preservation

Grażyna's portrait is the emotional anchor.

Preserve:

* facial proportions
* hairstyle
* glasses
* expression
* typography portrait

Do not stylize beyond the approved artistic direction.

---

# Performance

* smooth scrolling
* responsive layout
* GPU-friendly animations
